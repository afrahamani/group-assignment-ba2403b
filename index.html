About Snow Town Cafe

"Dessert & Sweetie Cafeteria" is a popular dining and leisure cafe in Kota Bharu, Kelantan, offering a variety of desserts, pastry sweeties, cooked foods, and beverages. It has been a popular choice since the early 20th century, known for its home-made recipes. The cafe has received positive market response in the east coast nation.

How we began

In 2019, engineer Mohd Hafiq Bin Razali, 27, opened the Snow Town Cafe in Pengkalan Chepa, Kota Bharu.With a passion for trendy desserts and pastry sweeties, he introduced his signature dishes to locals through self-studied recipes and local cuisine.
